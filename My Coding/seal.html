The Seals of Antarctica

Discover the incredible seals that thrive in the Earth's coldest continent.

Weddell Seal

These are known for their impressive underwater vocalizations and ability to dive deep beneath the ice.

Estimated Population: ~800,000

Source: IUCN (2015 assessment)
Harp Seal

Famous for their pure white pups, Harp seals are incredibly agile swimmers found in the icy waters of the North Atlantic and Arctic Oceans.

Estimated Population: 7.6 million

Source: NOAA Fisheries (2019)
Leopard Seal

As formidable apex predators, Leopard seals are known for their powerful jaws and serpentine appearance. They hunt penguins, fish, and even other seals.

Estimated Population: 220,000 - 440,000

Source: IUCN (2015 assessment)
